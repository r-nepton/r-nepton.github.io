CFHT: Instruments Updates and Upcoming projects

Laurie Rousseau-Nepton, Nadine Manset, Daniel Devost, Luc Arnold, Heather Flewelling and the QSO Team
CFHT is a ground-base 3.6 meter telescope located on the summit of the Maunakea in Hawaii serving the Canadian Communities since 1979. With large field of view imagers both in the Visible (MEGACAM) and the Infrared (WIRCAM), it makes good use of the exquisit image quality of the site. Two spectro-polarimeters offer high resolutions spectroscopy with polarimetric modes and a Fourrier transfor spectro-imager, SITELLE, allows to study with spectroscopy extended objects and structures.
